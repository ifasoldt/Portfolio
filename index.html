About Me

I'm a problem solver, a life-long learner, an adventure-seeker.

In retrospect, everything in my life has led up to me becoming a programmer. My whole life, I've had a natural curiousity and hunger to know why. I hate not understanding something, and when a subject picques my curiosity, I devote myself to learning all there is to know about it. Programming is a subject deep enought to occupy me for the rest of my life! My time spent teaching in the South Bronx has taught me to communicate and collaborate with people from completely differenty backgrounds than me. My degree in Philosophy enables me to break problems down into their constituent parts and create logical, coherent APIs, data-structures, and websites.

The back-end engineering course at the Iron Yard has given me a great foundation. Now, I'm excited to use my skills to contribute in the workplace, and to increase my knowledge and abilities on the job.
